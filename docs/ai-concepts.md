# AI & Machine Learning Concepts

## What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It encompasses:

### Types of AI
1. **Narrow AI (ANI)**: Designed for specific tasks (Siri, Alexa)
2. **General AI (AGI)**: Human-level intelligence (theoretical)
3. **Super AI (ASI)**: Surpasses human capabilities (future concept)

## Machine Learning Basics

Machine learning is a subset of AI where computers learn from data.

### Main Approaches

#### Supervised Learning
Learning from labeled data:
- Classification (spam vs not spam)
- Regression (predicting house prices)

#### Unsupervised Learning
Finding patterns in unlabeled data:
- Clustering (customer segmentation)
- Dimensionality reduction (PCA)

#### Reinforcement Learning
Learning through trial and reward:
- Game playing (AlphaGo)
- Robotics
- Self-driving cars

## Deep Learning

Deep learning uses neural networks with many layers:

### Popular Architectures
- **CNN**: For image recognition
- **RNN/LSTM**: For sequence data
- **Transformers**: For NLP (GPT, BERT)

## Practical Applications
- Chatbots & Virtual Assistants
- Recommendation systems (Netflix, Spotify)
- Image & speech recognition
- Fraud detection
- Medical diagnosis
- Autonomous vehicles

## Ethics in AI
Important considerations:
- Bias in algorithms
- Privacy concerns
- Job displacement
- Transparency & explainability
- Accountability
